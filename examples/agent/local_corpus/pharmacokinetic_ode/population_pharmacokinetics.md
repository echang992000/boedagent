# Population pharmacokinetics of oral X

We fit the absorption rate k_a and elimination rate k_e to a dataset of 40 patients and report LogNormal priors (mu=-1.2, sigma=0.4) for k_a and LogNormal(mu=-2.0, sigma=0.6) for k_e.
The reported range for k_a was 0.05 to 3.1 1/hr.
We used variational Bayesian experimental design (BOED) with expected information gain (EIG) as the objective.
